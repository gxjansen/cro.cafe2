Clear Service Worker Cache

This page will help you clear the service worker and its caches that are causing the offline page to show.

Manual Instructions (if the button doesn't work):

  1. Open Chrome DevTools (F12 or right-click → Inspect)
  2. Go to the Application tab
  3. In the left sidebar, click on Service Workers
  4. Find the service worker for localhost
  5. Click Unregister
  6. Still in the Application tab, click on Storage
  7. Click Clear site data
  8. Refresh the page
